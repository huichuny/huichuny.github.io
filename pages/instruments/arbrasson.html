Touching is a practice of grounding and breathing through pressure and sound. Arbrasson is a notched wooden instrument that resonates like a singing bird when its carved ridges are caressed. Through real-time computer processing, touch transforms into something spatial like fog, warm like a fire built from wood, and moist with memory like a flowing river.

making an arbrasson

femi was teaching me making an arbrasson at new machine today. we started from drawing lines on a piece of wood, then use a wood saw to carve gaps, then smooth it out with sanding paper, then fine tune it with hand saw. it took only few hours to finish.
