Artist Bio

Huichun Yang is a Taiwanese sound performer based in Providence, Rhode Island. Her electroacoustic practice weaves computer code, spectral analysis, and spatial sound with daxophones, arbrassons, and found objects, tracing a line between wild improvisation and deep listening. Electronics and wood become soil, and sound unfolds as thresholds where her body breathes, strains, and listens. Huichun has taught live coding at the Rhode Island School of Design and has performed internationally at ICMC (Boston), Non-Event (Boston), Residual Noise (Providence), EESF (Los Angeles), and the SOUND/IMAGE Festival (London). She received a Foundation for Contemporary Arts grant in 2025.
