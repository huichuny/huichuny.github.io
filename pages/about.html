Artist Biography

Huichun Yang is a Taiwanese sound performer based in Providence, Rhode Island. Her electroacoustic practice merges improvisation, spatial audio, and computer music programming. She builds instruments such as the arbrasson, daxophone, balloons and mouth harps, integrating them with electronics that respond to her body and movement. Performing in natural spaces, she invites listeners to attune to resonance within space and breath. Her work has been presented internationally at EESF (Los Angeles), ICMC (Boston), Non-Event (Boston), Residual Noise (Providence), and the SOUND/IMAGE Festival (London). She received a Foundation for Contemporary Arts grant in 2025.
