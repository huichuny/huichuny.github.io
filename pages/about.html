Huichun Yang (楊惠鈞) is a Taiwanese sound performer based in Providence, Rhode Island. Her work moves through breath, body, and environment via spatial audio and electroacoustic performance. She builds and plays wooden instruments, balloons, and mouth harps, integrating them with electronics and computer-based sound programming. Her practice reflects on the in-between spaces of air, land, breath, and touch.

Huichun’s performances have been presented at EESF (Los Angeles, 2025), ICMC (Boston, 2025), Non-Event (Boston, 2025), Residual Noise (Providence, 2025), and the SOUND/IMAGE Festival (London, 2024). She received a grant from the Foundation for Contemporary Arts in 2025.
