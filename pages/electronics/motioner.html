Glove wirelessly controls the ambisonic signal of IEM plugin in MaxMSP.

This project uses

  • ESP32
  • MPU6050
  • 3.7v Battery
  • TP-Link Wi-Fi Router

The ESP32, wifi router, and my computer are connected to the same IP address. Once they are connected, data from the MPU6050, which is a 3-axis gyroscope and a 3-axis accelerometer, can be sent to my Max patch and move the postion of stereo signal in the IEM plugin.

I was experiment with the duration of the balloon and different gesture with the gloves that control the spatial sound position.
